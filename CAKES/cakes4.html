Black Forest cake

Indulge in the divine delight of homemade eggless Black Forest Cake with our easy recipe. The cake prepared with Nestlé MILKMAID is rich, moist, and luscious, and offers an unmatchable melt-in-the-mouth texture. And then, you bite into cherries, and their tartness cuts through the sweetness, creating this amazing balance of flavours. And let's not forget the whipped cream—it's light and airy, adding a creamy touch that makes each bite memorable.

Ingredients of Black Forest cake:-
  1. 1 tin Nestlé MILKMAID
  2. 1 (25g) Nestlé Milk Chocolate
  3. 100g Butter
  4. 1¾ cup (175 g) Maida
  5. 3-4 heaped tbsp (25g) Cocoa
  6. 1 tsp Baking Powder
  7. 1 tsp Soda Bicarbonate
  8. 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  9. 1-1/3 cups (200ml) Aerated soda
  10. 1 small tin Tinned Cherries
  11. 200g Cream
  12. 2 tsp Icing Sugar/Powdered Sugar

Directions:

  1. Grease an 8” diameter baking tin and dust it with flour.
  2. Pre heat the oven at 180o C. Sieve together maida, cocoa powder, baking powder and soda bicarbonate (sieve 2-3 times for better aeration).
  3. Soften butter (DO NOT MELT). Add Nestlé MILKMAID and beat well. Add the vanilla essence. Add maida mixture and aerated soda alternately to the batter till all the maida and soda are used up.
  4. Pour the batter into the baking tin (8” diameter) and bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes or till the toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven, cool for a while. Loosen sides of cake, using a knife if necessary. Turnout over a wire rack/plate lined with cloth (to absorb moisture) and cool slightly before cutting horizontally into 2.
  6. Drain & destone cherries. Soak both cakes halves with cherry syrup. Whip cream and sugar till light and fluffy.Sandwich the two layers of cake with whipped cream and chopped cherries.
  7. Top with whipped cream and cherries. Sprinkle generously with grated chocolate. Chill and serve.

Tips and Variations:

  1. Use high-quality ingredients, especially for the chocolate, cherries, and whipped cream, as they significantly impact the final taste of the Black Forest Cake.
  2. Accurate measurements are crucial for baking. Use measuring cups and spoons to ensure you add the right amount of each ingredient.
  3. When incorporating dry ingredients into the batter, gently fold them in using a spatula. Avoid overmixing to maintain a light and fluffy texture.
  4. Allow the cake layers to cool completely before assembling and frosting. This prevents the frosting from melting and ensures a stable structure.
  5. Use chilled cream and a cold mixing bowl to whip the cream.

Taste: Sweet and chocolaty with soft and moist texture.

Serving Ideas:

Serve it as a dessert after dinner or make it for the kid’s birthday party. Enjoy it with vanilla ice-cream or on its own.
