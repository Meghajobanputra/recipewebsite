Brownies cake

Ingredients of Brownies cake:-
  1. 1/2 tin Nestlé MILKMAID
  2. 1/2 cup (75ml) Nestlé a+ Toned Milk
  3. 125 gms Maida (All-Purpose Flour)
  4. 50 gms Cocoa Powder
  5. 1 tsp Baking Powder
  6. 125 gms Butter
  7. 75 gms Sugar Powdered
  8. 2 Eggs
  9. 1/2 cup Chopped Walnuts

Directions:

  1. Grease a baking tin & dust with maida. Preheat oven to 180o C. Sieve together maida, cocoa powder & baking powder.
  2. Beat butter & sugar until light & creamy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Fold in the sieved ingredients of step 1 into the above mixture, alternating with the milk and Nestlé MILKMAID. Add chopped walnuts. Mix well & pour into a greased tin.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the center of cake springs back when lightly pressed. Allow to cool in the tin for 5 minutes and remove onto a wire rack. Cut as desired.

Tips and Variations:

  1. Grease the baking tin and keep the oven preheated and ready.
  2. The maida, cocoa and baking powder must be sieved together.
  3. The butter and sugar mix must be light and creamy to ensure that the brownies fluff up well.
  4. Make sure that you add the eggs one at a time, beating them well after each addition.
  5. Add the sieved ingredients into the mixture, alternating with the milk and Nestlé MILKMAID. Ensure that there are no lumps.
  6. Make sure to wait for the brownies to cool down before cutting them.

Taste: Sweet and chocolaty with soft and moist texture.

Serving Ideas:

Serve it as a dessert after dinner or make it for the kid’s birthday party. Enjoy it with vanilla ice-cream or on its own.
