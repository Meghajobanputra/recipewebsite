Mint Chip Ice Cream

This mint chocolate chip ice cream recipe is one that I discovered by accident. I think the 2% milk makes the ice cream taste lighter.

Ingredients of Mint Chip Ice Cream:-
  1. 2 cups 2% milk
  2. 2 cups heavy cream
  3. 1 cup sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. 1 teaspoon peppermint extract
  6. Â½ teaspoon salt
  7. 3 drops green food coloring (Optional)
  8. 1 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Stir milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, peppermint extract, and salt together in a large bowl until the sugar has dissolved. Mix in green food coloring.
  2. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
  3. Add chocolate chips about 10 minutes into the freezing. Continue churning until ice cream has thickened, about 20 minutes more.
  4. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm, about 2 hours.

Tips and Variations:

  1. 3-quart stainless steel saucier, non-reactive fine mesh strainer, ice cream maker, non-reactive, freezer-safe container
  2. After adding fruit to the puree, immediately stir the mixture for 3-minutes to prevent lump formation.
  3. You can take an equal amount of mixture of raspberry and mint chocolate chip to prepare a mixed fruit freezer jam.

Taste: Sweet and natural taste of mint chocolate chip.

Serving Ideas:

Historically, mint chocolate chip has always been lowfat by nature, because it was a byproduct of churning butter. Modern manufacturing methods may not produce buttermilk the same way, but low-fat styles best mimic the flavor and body prized in the original.
